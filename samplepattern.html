Boba Snail

Medium
3.5mm
Worsted
Animal

Abbreviations

  • sc - single crochet
  • inc - increase
  • dec - decrease
  • st - stitch
  • ch - chain
  • sl st - slip stitch
  • blo - back loop only

Materials

  • Yarn A (Cream)
  • Yarn B (White)
  • Yarn C (Purple)
  • Yarn D (Yellow)

  • 12mm Safety Eyes
  • 3.5mm crochet hook
  • Stuffing
  • Yarn needle
  • Scissors
  • Stitch Marker
  • Brown Felt
  • Black Embroidery Thread

  • PATTERN SOURCE

Instructions

Body (Yarn A)

  • R1 - sc6 in a magic ring (6)
  • R2 - inc x 6 (12)
  • R3 - (sc, inc) x 6 (18)
  • R4 - (sc 2, inc) x 6 (24)
  • R5 - (sc 3, inc) x 6 (30)
  • R6 to R32 - sc all around (30)
  • After R18, add eyes between R11, and R12, 5 st's apart. Lightly stuff up to R17.
  • R33 - (sc 3, dec) x 6 (24)
  • R34 to R38 - sc all around (24)
  • R39 - (sc 2, dec) x 6 (18)
  • R40 to R41 - sc all around (18)
  • R42 - (sc, dec) x 6 (12)
  • R43 - sc all around (12)
  • R44 - dec x 6 (6)
  • Finish off, close hole

Antenna (Yarn B) - Make 2

  • R1 - Ch 8
  • R2 - sl st in second ch from hook, sl st to the end (7)

Shell (Start w/ Yarn B)

  • R1 - sc6 in a magic ring (6)
  • R2 - inc x 6 (12)
  • R3 - (sc, inc) x 6 (18)
  • R4 - (sc 2, inc) x 6 (24)
  • R5 - (sc 3, inc) x 6 (30)
  • R6 - (sc 4, inc) x 6 (36)
  • Switch to Yarn C
  • R6 - (sc 5, inc) x 6 (42)
  • R8 to R15 - sc all around x 6 (42)
  • R16 - (sc 5, dec) x 6 (36)
  • R17 - (sc 4, dec) x 6 (30)
  • R18 - (sc 3, dec) x 6 (24)
  • R19 - (sc 2, dec) x 6 (18)
  • Add stuffing
  • R20 - (sc, dec) x 6 (12)
  • R21 - dec x 6 (6)
  • Add more stuffing if needed. Leave a long tail for sewing and close hole

Straw (Yarn D)

  • R1 - sc 8 in a magic ring (8)
  • R2 - sc around in BLO (8)
  • R3 - R7 - sc all around (8)
  • Leave a long tail for sewing, do not close. Add stuffing

Assembly

  • Cut boba perls from brown felt. Glue around boba shell
  • Sew antennas on top snail's head
  • Sew bottom of the boba shell onto the snail boy
  • Sew straw on top of boba shell
  • Use pink felt/pink yarn/blush to add cheeks. Use black embroidery thread to make a mouth
